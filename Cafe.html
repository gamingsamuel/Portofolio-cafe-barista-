About Cafe Barista

At Cafe Barista, we believe in the art of coffee-making. From selecting the finest beans to perfecting each cup, our expert baristas deliver meaningful coffee experiences. Enjoy a cozy atmosphere, friendly service, and unique blends tailored just for you.

Our Coffee Specialties

Espresso

Strong and bold espresso shots brewed to perfection, the heart of many coffee drinks.

Cafe Latte

Smooth espresso combined with steamed milk, topped with velvety foam and artistic designs.

Cold Brew

Slow steeped cold brew, smooth and less acidic, perfect to refresh on warm days.

Cappuccino

Balanced espresso with foamed milk and a sprinkle of cinnamon or cocoa powder.

Mocha

A harmonious combination of espresso, chocolate, and steamed milk topped with whipped cream.

Flat White

Silky microfoam with rich espresso, delivering a creamy texture and bold flavor.
